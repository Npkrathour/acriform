ARCIFORM is a well-respected design-build firm primarily serving the Portland area.

Since 1997, we’ve served our long list of commercial and residential clients with experience and integrity. At ARCIFORM, we are inspired by the stories that surround the structures we design, build, and renovate. Whether a historic landmark or your family’s beloved home, we learn the stories behind each structure that help preserve their cultural and architectural heritage while bringing them up to modern standards of safety, comfort, and ease of use.

Making meaning and forging real human connections are part of ARCIFORM’s process when working with clients. At the heart of our business is the belief that these stories and our clients matter.

We believe that design-build partnerships are the best option for both the client and the contractor.

Our designers collaborate with the project managers to identify hidden costs and make choices that provide the best value. Through this process, we can meet our clients’ building needs and work according to their budget. Best of all, we operate as a collaborative team, which turns challenging projects into positive experiences.

The quality of our work is enhanced by our crew’s considerable experience and caring attitude. We maintain a primary group of designers, project managers, and key crew members for reliable and consistent production. As a self-executing company, we do most of our own work, avoiding subcontracting work that contractors could be doing themselves. Keeping work in-house when possible provides quality control and ensures that projects are completed in a timely manner.
