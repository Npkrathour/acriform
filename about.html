AT ARCIFORM WE ARE INSPIRED BY THE STORIES OF PEOPLE AND SPACES

Our philosophy is simple – we help our clients create incredible spaces that reflect their unique story. At the heart of our business is the belief that these stories and our clients matter. We know how stressful a remodel or construction can be, so we’ve created an approach that makes this process as positive as possible. From the initial plan to the final walk-through, we’ve put together a team of experts that’s committed to their craft and committed to our customers.

The result is a difference you’ll see and feel. The ARCIFORM Way is a commitment to exceptional service and personal care as well as a commitment to quality, detail and beautiful results. We look forward to showing you just how different your experience will be when it’s done The ARCIFORM Way.

HISTORY

Since 1997, we’ve been listening to what is in the walls and floorboards, joists and moldings, and combining it with our clients’ personalities and visions. We’ve been taking a page from history and writing a story all its own. With respect for the past and a vision for the future, we create a story of crafted, common sense solutions and buildable projects.

WHAT TO EXPECT

At ARCIFORM, we build to last. It’s one of our core values. That’s why we’ve built a process based on a clear understanding of your goals and have created a path to help you achieve those goals. Here’s what you can expect as we kick off our engagement…

DEVELOP

To bring that vision to life, we create a plan. In this stage, our team of designers and builders will collaborate with you to develop concepts and buildable plans based on our goals, priorities and budget. Developing an aesthetic concept helps us create beautiful spaces inspired by your story!

DOCUMENT

To make sure we’re tracking to your goals, our design and build teams will develop or review a complete set of plans and detailed specs. From there our build team will develop work orders for your review and approval.

SUSTAIN

Our engagement doesn’t end when your project does. We want to make sure you’re as happy about the final product as we are. We consider you and your project a part of our story and will check in with you and look forward to hearing your feedback!

CONSULT

We want to make sure you know what to expect when you engage with ARCIFORM. We start every project with a consultation where we create alignment on goals and next steps in the process. You can count on us to continue checking in on those goals and making sure we’re aligned on an ongoing basis. Understanding your story starts with understanding you. We’re experts at bringing our client’s dreams to life. Our specialists will meet with you to understand your aesthetic, ideas, and vision and be ready to talk feasibility, budgets and time frames. BUILD

Our talented craftsmen and women are now working hard to make your dreams come true.
